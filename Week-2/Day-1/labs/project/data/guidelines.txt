Hypertension:
- BP > 140 indicates Stage 1 Hypertension
- Treatment:
  • Lifestyle modification
  • ACE inhibitors or beta blockers

Diabetes:
- High blood sugar levels
- Treatment:
  • Insulin therapy
  • Metformin

Cardiac:
- Risk of heart disease
- Treatment:
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Medication and monitoring

Normal:
- No immediate risk
- Maintain healthy lifestyle
