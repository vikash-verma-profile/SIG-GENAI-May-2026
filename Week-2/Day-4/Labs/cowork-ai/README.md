# CoWork AI Productivity Automation (Meeting Notes → Actions + Email)

## What you have to do (from `CoWork AI Productivity Automation.docx`)

Scenario: daily engineering meetings lead to missed action items due to manual note-taking.

You will use GenAI to:
- Convert meeting notes into structured action items (owner, deadline, risk, reminders)
- Generate a professional stakeholder email summary

## Sample meeting notes (from the lab)

- Data ingestion pipeline failed due to schema mismatch.
- Rahul will fix ADF mapping by tomorrow.
- Priya to validate Snowflake tables.
- DevOps team will increase Kubernetes memory limits.
- Client demo scheduled Friday.

## Task 1 — Action items prompt (use this)

> Convert the following meeting notes into:
> 1. Action items
> 2. Owners
> 3. Deadlines
> 4. Risks
> 5. Follow-up reminders

## Task 2 — Email summary prompt (use this)

> Generate a professional email summary from the following engineering meeting notes.
